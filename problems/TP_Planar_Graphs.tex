\begin{problem}

%% type: short-answer
%% title: Planar Graphs

A planar graph has 7 more edges than vertices.  How many faces does it
have?

\begin{solution}
9

$v - e + f = 2$, so
$f = (e - v ) + 2 = 7 + 2$.
\end{solution}
