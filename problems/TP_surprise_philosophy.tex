\begin{problem}
Why does the ``surprise'' paradox \inbook{of
  Problem~\bref{CP_surprise_quiz_next_week}} present a philosophical
problem but not a mathematical one?

\begin{solution}
The concept of ``surprise,'' like similar concepts such as ``knowing''
or ``believing'' have no satisfactory mathematical definitions.
\end{solution}

\end{problem}
