\begin{problem}  \textbf{Fill in the table with T/F}

For the two logical formulas below, write true (T) or false (F) in each
box in the table to indicate the truth value of the formula under the
listed domains.  No justification is needed.

\begin{center}
  \begin{tabular}{| c | c | c | c |}
    \hline
    Formula & Odd Integers & Reals & Complexes \\
    \hline
    & & & \\
    $\forall x \exists y (x^2 + y^2 = 1)$ & & & \\ 
    & & & \\
    \hline
    & & & \\
    $\forall x \forall y (x^2 + y^2 > 0)$ & & & \\ 
    & & & \\
    \hline  
    \end{tabular}
\end{center}

\begin{solution}
\begin{center}
  \begin{tabular}{| c | c | c | c |}
    \hline
    Formula & Odd Integers & Reals & Complexes \\
    \hline
    & & & \\
    $\forall x \exists y (x^2 + y^2 = 1)$ & F & F & T \\ 
    & & & \\
    \hline
    & & & \\
    $\forall x \forall y (x^2 + y^2 > 0)$ & T & F & F \\ 
    & & & \\
    \hline  
    \end{tabular}
\end{center}
\end{solution}

\end{problem}
