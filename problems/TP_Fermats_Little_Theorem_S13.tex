\begin{problem}
What is $\rem{24^{79}}{79}$?

\hint  You should not need to do any actual multiplications!  

\begin{solution}
\textbf{24}.

Note that 79 is prime.

Now Fermat's Little Theorem says that $n^{78}= 1 \inzmod{79}$ for all $n
\in (0,79)$.  So
\[
24^{79} = 24 \cdot 24^{78} = 24 \cdot 1 = 24 \inzmod{79},
\]
which is equivalent to the assertion that $\rem{24^{79}}{79} =24$.

\end{solution}

\end{problem}
