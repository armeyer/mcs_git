\begin{problem} \mbox{}

Circle the invalid implication, and provide a counter model to prove its invalidity. 
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\forall x, \exists y. P(x, y) \implies \exists y, \forall x. P(x, y)$
\item $\exists y, \forall x. P(x, y) \implies \forall x, \exists y. P(x, y)$
\end{enumerate}

\end{problem}
